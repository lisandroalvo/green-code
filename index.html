Sustainable UX/UI design with Lisandro

Hi, I’m Lisandro, your motivator to fall in love with sustainable web design.
I’ve been supporting businesses and change makers like you to design user-friendly, accessible and ecologically sustainable websites, apps, shops and other digital products. We as businesses and creators have a responsibility. Our websites shape today’s society.
They frustrate or inspire. They have an impact on our planet. And it is precisely this responsibility that we have to use wisely. Together. Towards Environment Centered Design.
